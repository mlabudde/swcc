Club Activities Suspended

Due to COVID-19 the SWCC has suspended all activities

Like many other other organizations, the Southwest Chess Club is doing its part to help contain the spread of the Coronavirus by cancelling all club meeting dates until further notice. The Hales Corners Challenge XXXI, which was scheduled for April 4, has been cancelled. Please check back in May and follow the guidelines and recommendations of the CDC and other health organizations.

Meeting Location Changes during the remainder of 2020

The majority of Thursdays we will continue to meet at the Village Hall in Hales Corners. However, we have a couple of remaining dates when we plan to meet at 600 W. Virginia St (near south side of Milwaukee).

Welcome to the Southwest Chess Club!

The Southwest Chess Club meets every Thursday night at the
Hales Corners Village Hall /Police Station
5635 South New Berlin Road, Hales Corners, Wisconsin
in the downstairs Community Room.
The club opens at 6 PM and Tournament Games begin at 7 PM.

We have a Blog that shows current club news, and you are invited to contribute information, games, and any items of interest. You can subscribe to the blog and receive email notification of new events or announcements!
